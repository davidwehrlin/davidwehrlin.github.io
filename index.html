About

He found the chocolate covered roaches quite tasty. He is no James Bond; his name is Roger Moore. Pantyhose and heels are an interesting choice of attire for the beach. I’m a living furnace. Before he moved to the inner city, he had always believed that security complexes were psychological. It was the scarcity that fueled his creativity. Joe made the sugar cookies; Susan decorated them. I'd rather be a bird than a fish. Greetings from the real universe. He set out for a short walk, but now all he could see were mangroves and water were for miles.
